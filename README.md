I'm Fran. I help run [Ortaggi](https://ortaggi.co.uk) — wholesale fruit and veg out of [New Covent Garden Market](https://www.newcoventgardenmarket.com/) in London. Most weeks I'm doing two jobs in parallel: pre-dawn shifts picking on the market floor, and writing the software that runs the rest of the business between deliveries.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/franko-c/franko-c/main/profile-divider.svg" alt="section divider" width="100%" />

### What I do at Ortaggi

The day-to-day is basically everything that isn't growing the food:

- Overnight market runs at NCGM — picking, checking quality, hauling.
- Finding new customers — cold outreach, restaurant visits, walking into kitchens.
- Negotiating with suppliers and growers at market.
- Admin: orders, dispatch, pricing, invoices, the unsexy half of a wholesale business.
- Building out a cargo-bike delivery route into central London offices — produce hand-picked overnight, dropped off before desks fill up.

### What I build for it

Whatever software stops me doing the same admin twice.

- **[ortaggi-lead-gen](https://github.com/franko-c/ortaggi-lead-gen)** — internal CRM + lead pipeline. Scans Google Places for restaurants, offices, care homes; enriches with FSA ratings + cuisine signals; pre-call briefings via Gemini; map-first dashboard for visit planning; personalised outreach engine.
- **[ortaggi-instagram-studio](https://github.com/franko-c/ortaggi-instagram-studio)** — content generator so the brand stays alive without me copywriting at 2am.
- **[wholesale-landing](https://github.com/franko-c/wholesale-landing)** — the public Ortaggi front door.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/franko-c/franko-c/main/profile-divider-2.svg" alt="section divider" width="100%" />

### Side quests

Stuff that started as personal itches and grew teeth:

- **orbital-discord** — AI-native community ops platform for Discord. Was meant to scratch the "answering the same question in five servers" itch; now it's a real thing.
- **second-brain** — personal PWA: capture, classify, tasks, habits, finance, calendar. Next 16 + Cloudflare Workers + D1.
- **dar1en-zwift-server-helper** — small utility for the Zwift cycling rig. Niche, but it scratches the right nerve.
- Older bits: a leads_tracker prototype, a few TOP coursework repos, scoreboards, a recipe site.
